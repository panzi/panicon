1. If you move your mouse you will see a panner in the right bottom corner of the page.
  2. Grab and drag the little rectangle to move pan around quickly.
  3. Grab anywhere on the panner with the middle mouse button or the left mouse button while holding shift in order to slowly pan (requires the browser support fo the mouse lock API).
  4. Or click anywhere in the panner to jump to that location.
  5. That's all! See GitHub for the source.
